About

Our Humble Beginnings

Every great idea starts with a problem. When we were students at Univeristy of Pennslyvania, one of us lost a pair of glasses and spent the rest of the semester squinting and complaining. We identified that the eyewear industry was dominated by expensive name-brands that provided inaccessible and unaffordable glasses.

Solution

We decided to create an alternative. Warby Parker is involved in every process of the creation of these glasses: we source our own material, acetate; design fashionable glasses in-house; hand-assemble every pair of glasses; engage with our customers directly; and deliver these glasses right to our customers' doorsteps. We are able to provide higher quality, better looking prescription eye at an cheaper price.

Present

Warby Parker is an ever-evolving company. From listening to our customers, we found that other problems arises. Thus, we diversified our products and services to address these common problems found among our customers.

  • We found that our customers lack the time to go into physical stores to try on frames. Thus, we have a home try-on program where we deliver five frames for customers to try at home.
  • In addition, we created the virtual try-on program on our app for our customers to try-on frames immediately on their smartphones.
  • We found that customers were complaining about the bulkiness of the packaging of contact lenses. Thus, we created Scout by Warby Parker, affordable, comforable, contact lenses that comes in space-saving flat packs. It allows customers to buy and order contact lenses online.
  • We found that customers with busy schedules didn't have time to go into eye clinics for eye exams. Thus, we created an app that gives eye test online. We partnered up with doctors to assess customer's visual acuity after the eye exam.

Future

Warby Parker thrives to become an one-stop shop for all of our customers eyecare needs. From contact lenses, to sunglasses, to prescription glasses, we've got you covered. We will continue to notice your needs and find a solution to accomodate it.

Products

Thriving to be an one-stop shop for all of our customers' eyecare needs

Prescription Glasses

Warby Parker offers more than 100 styles of cheap prescription glasses. The glasses are made from cellulose acetate and other materials and are designed and hand-crafted by our in-house engineers. Frames can be customized for fit based on the width of our customer's faces and the height of their nose bridge. Some of the frames are eligible for our home try-on program.

Sunglasses

Like our cheap prescription glasses, we have many styles of sunglasses. Since most of our production are in-house, we are able to mix and match frames with the types of lenses customers want.We offer different shapes of frames including round, square, rectangle, cat-eye and aviator. Sunglasses are eligible to have progressive lens, reader lens, single version lens and non-prescrption lens. All the lens blocks 100% of UV rays and are scratch resistant.

Contacts

Scout by Warby Parker is our newest product line. After hearing complaints about bulky contact lenses packaging, we've developed a line of contact lenses in flat pack space-saving packaging. The packaging uses 80% less packaging than traditional blister packs, making it more eco-friendly. Warby Parker allow you to order contacts online after having your prescription verified. It is an one-stop shop to get buy contact lenses, sunglasses and glasses online.

Eye Exams

We understand that eye exams at your local clinic is a long process. To skip the line, the scheduling and the walk to your clinic, we offer eye test online. The test online is done virtually. Simply put your devices at a distance as instructed and follow the displayed instructions. Your result will be assessed by a doctor and verified. The online eye exam help save time and troubles. With the eye exam results, the eyewear specialists can help find glasses that best fit your needs.

Home Try-on

Again, visitng a local glasses store can take a long time. We understand that it is hard to take the time out of your day and go out of your way to try-on glasses in stores. Therefore, we created the home try-on program. You can pick out five frames that you want to try out and we will deliver the frames to your house. Customers can take as long as they need to try the frames on. The program tries to migate the pressure and awkwardness of trying frames on in the store. We want to bring you our frames without you having to leave the comfort of your home.

Virtual Try-on

Maybe you didn't like the five pairs of frames from the home try-on program or you wanted to try the frames on immediately without any time lag, we got you! We have developed an virtual try-on program in the form of an app that let you try on frames immediately. The app works like an Instagram or Snapchat filter. The app would utilize your smartphone camera and hover the desired frame over your face. The virtual try-on program is a great way to be experimental with new frame shapes and to have fun with some of our funky frames!
